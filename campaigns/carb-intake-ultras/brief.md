## Thesis

The research on endurance fueling moved from 60 grams of carbohydrate per hour to 90-120 with the right ratios.  Most athletes are still fueling to the old number.

## Audience

Ultra-runners, Ironman triathletes, endurance cyclists, coaches.

## Key Messages

1. Old rule: 60g/hour.  New ceiling: 90-120g/hour with 2:1 glucose-fructose ratio.
2. The difference shows up in the final third of any long effort.
3. Gut absorption is trainable.  Four to six weeks of progressive fueling.
