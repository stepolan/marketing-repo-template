# Summit Fuel Corporate Voice Guidelines

## Core Attributes
- **Evidence-based** — Every claim backed by research or lived experience
- **Practical** — Real advice for real athletes
- **Honest about limits** — We say when we do not know
- **Not bro-y** — No hype, no "unlock your potential," no fake urgency
- **Respectful of the reader** — Our audience is smart.  We write to that.

## The #1 Rule: Never Bury the Lead

Every piece of content must lead with the most interesting, surprising, or valuable insight.  Not after context.  Not after the journey.  First.

**Test:** If someone reads only the first 3 sentences and walks away, did they get the main point?  If not, restructure.

**Common traps to avoid:**
- **Journey trap:** "First we tried X, then Y, then we discovered Z."  Lead with Z.
- **Context trap:** "To understand this, you need to know A, B, C."  State the point, then provide context.
- **Fairness trap:** "There are many valid approaches..." before getting to your actual position.  Take the position first.
